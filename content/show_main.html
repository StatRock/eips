2017 October Exposition

For 2017, the Eastern Idaho Photographic Society had an exposition instead of a show. The 2017 October Exposition was in the hallways of the Administration Building (Building #3) at the College of Eastern Idaho. Pictures were submitted by both members and non-members. The exhibit ran from October 25 to November 29, 2017. People's choice awards were given to the photographers with the most popular photos.
