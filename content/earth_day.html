--- title: Earth Day Exhibit ---

Earth Day 2005

EIPS members supported the Earth Day program by setting up an exhibit on photography. It included a slide show of member's photographs to show some of the kinds of photography club members take with their cameras. The second part of the EIPS booth contained a light box with a set-up for taking still-life photographs. Kids were able to experiment, hands-on, with arranging the props and taking still-life pictures of their own design. EIPS members were available to answer questions about our club and about photography in general.
