Idaho Falls Magazine Photo Contest

The Idaho Falls Magazine has a People's Choice: East Idaho Community Photo Contest each year. The deadline is typically in early November for submitting pictures that will compete in the following year. People vote on each entered picture, and the ones with the highest average scores win.

The EIPS had 4 members win in this contest in recent years (2014-2016). Michael Fryer won Best in Show in 2014.
