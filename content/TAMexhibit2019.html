2019 EIPS Exhibit at the Art Museum of Eastern Idaho

EIPS has an upcoming photo exhibit with the Art Museum. Our photos will be displayed in the Studio Gallery from May 28 through July 6, 2019. EIPS photographers will deliver pictures to the museum during the week of May 20-25. Call Steve at 208-709-8580 if you have any questions.

The museum will have a reception for us during the Artwalk on Thursday, June 6th from 5:00-8:00 p.m. We hope everyone comes to support our exhibit.
